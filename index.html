Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web development, app creation, and digital marketing services.

Web Dev What technologies do you use for web development?

We use modern technologies like React.js, Next.js, Vue.js for frontend, and Node.js, Python (Django/Flask), PHP (Laravel) for backend. For CMS solutions, we work with WordPress, Shopify, and headless options like Contentful or Strapi. Our stack is always updated to industry standards.

We choose technologies based on your project requirements, scalability needs, and budget considerations.

Web Dev How long does a typical website take to build?

Development timelines vary based on complexity:

  • Basic brochure website: 2-4 weeks
  • Corporate website: 4-8 weeks
  • E-commerce site: 6-12 weeks
  • Custom web application: 8-16+ weeks

We provide a detailed timeline after our initial discovery session.

App Dev Do you develop native or cross-platform apps?

We develop both native and cross-platform applications based on your needs:

Native Apps: Swift for iOS, Kotlin for Android - Best performance and access to all device features

Cross-Platform: Flutter or React Native - Faster development, single codebase for both platforms

We'll recommend the best approach after analyzing your requirements, target audience, and budget.

App Dev What's included in your app maintenance plans?

Our maintenance plans typically include:

  • Regular bug fixes and updates
  • OS compatibility updates
  • Performance optimization
  • Security patches
  • App store compliance updates
  • 24/7 monitoring (premium plans)
  • Monthly analytics reports

Plans are customizable based on your app's needs.

Digital Marketing How do you measure SEO success?

We track multiple KPIs to measure SEO performance:

  • Organic traffic growth (Google Analytics)
  • Keyword rankings for target phrases
  • Click-through rates from search results
  • Domain authority improvements
  • Conversion rates from organic traffic
  • Backlink profile growth and quality

We provide monthly reports with actionable insights.

Digital Marketing What's your PPC campaign management process?

Our proven PPC process includes:

  1. Strategy Development: Goal setting, audience targeting, budget planning
  2. Campaign Setup: Account structure, keyword research, ad creation
  3. Launch & Optimization: Bid adjustments, A/B testing, negative keywords
  4. Conversion Tracking: Goal setup, attribution modeling
  5. Ongoing Management: Weekly optimizations, monthly reporting

We focus on maximizing ROI, not just clicks.

General What's your project pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models:

Fixed Price: For well-defined projects with clear requirements

Time & Materials: For projects needing flexibility during development

Retainer Model: For ongoing maintenance and support

Dedicated Team: For long-term projects requiring full-time resources

After understanding your project scope, we'll recommend the most cost-effective approach.

General How do you ensure project security and confidentiality?

We take security seriously with multiple measures:

  • Signed NDAs before project discussions
  • Secure development environments
  • Regular security audits
  • Encrypted communications
  • Role-based access controls
  • Secure code repositories
  • Compliance with GDPR/CCPA

We can also sign additional confidentiality agreements specific to your industry requirements.
